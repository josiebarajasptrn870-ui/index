Our story

Founded in 2020 by a collective of bakers and food scientists, Doventha began as a small workshop in a converted storefront. We quickly realized that the baking world lacked accessible, in-depth resources that respected both tradition and innovation. Today, we serve thousands of bakers worldwide — from home enthusiasts to professional pastry chefs.

Our methodology combines time-honored techniques with modern food science, giving you the "why" behind every step. We believe that understanding fermentation, gluten, and heat transforms baking from a recipe-following exercise into a true craft.

📖 Our philosophy

Content-first, skill-focused. Every guide, video, and workshop is built around core principles: ingredient quality, temperature control, and fermentation intelligence. We don't just give recipes — we give you the intuition to create your own.

🌾 Community & collaboration

We host monthly sourdough clinics, Q&A sessions, and collaborative bakes. Our online forum is a space for troubleshooting, sharing successes, and learning from fellow bakers across the globe.

About Doventha — FAQs

Is Doventha only for professional bakers?

Not at all. Our content is designed for home bakers, aspiring professionals, and anyone who wants to deepen their understanding of baking. We offer beginner-friendly guides alongside advanced techniques.

Do you offer in-person workshops?

Yes, we host seasonal workshops in our home studio (currently in the UK and Italy). We also offer fully virtual workshops with interactive Q&A.

How can I join the community?

Sign up for our newsletter to receive weekly insights, and join our forum (free) to connect with other bakers and ask questions.

Our Story

ZenithFact Network was founded in 2005 with a simple mission: to deliver accurate, unbiased news in a world increasingly filled with misinformation. What began as a small print publication has grown into a global multimedia news organization with bureaus in 12 countries and a digital reach that spans the globe.

Our name reflects our commitment to reaching the highest point of factual reporting. "Zenith" represents the peak or highest point, and "Fact" signifies our unwavering dedication to truth and accuracy in journalism.

Today, we continue to uphold the principles that guided our founders: integrity, accuracy, and a commitment to serving the public good through quality journalism.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To inform, educate, and empower our global audience through accurate, unbiased, and comprehensive news coverage that holds power to account and gives voice to the voiceless.

Our Vision

To create a better-informed world where citizens have access to truthful information that enables them to make decisions that improve their lives and communities.

Our Promise

To uphold the highest standards of journalistic integrity, ensuring that every story we publish is thoroughly researched, fact-checked, and presented with context and fairness.

Our Core Values

Accuracy

We prioritize factual correctness in every story, employing a multi-layered fact-checking process to ensure the information we deliver is reliable and trustworthy.

Impartiality

We present news without bias, providing balanced coverage that allows our audience to form their own opinions based on facts and diverse perspectives.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards, acknowledging errors when they occur and being transparent about our sources and methods.

Accountability

We hold powerful institutions and individuals to account, serving as a watchdog in the public interest and giving voice to those without power.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and storytelling methods to better serve our audience while maintaining our commitment to journalistic excellence.

Diversity

We strive to represent the diverse communities we serve, ensuring our coverage includes a wide range of perspectives and experiences.

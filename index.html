    Frequently asked questions

    How do I maintain my sourdough starter?

    Feed daily with equal parts flour and water (by weight). Store at room temperature or refrigerate for weekly feeding. Discard half before each feeding.

    Why is my bread dense?

    Under-fermentation, weak gluten, or insufficient steam. Check bulk fermentation time and dough windowpane. Ensure oven is preheated thoroughly.

    Can I bake without a Dutch oven?

    Yes, use a baking stone with a steam tray (ice cubes or water) at the bottom of the oven. Alternatively, use a heavy roasting pan covered with foil.

Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and help us improve our workshop offerings. This page explains what cookies we use and how you can manage them.

🍪 Last updated: June 28, 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your actions and preferences (like language or workshop preferences) over time, so you don’t have to keep re-entering them.

How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in (if you have an account)
  • Remember your workshop preferences and class selections
  • Understand how you interact with our content (analytics)
  • Improve site performance and user experience
🧁 Our promise: We never use cookies for targeted advertising or cross-site tracking. Your data stays with Doventha.

Types of cookies we use

We keep things minimal. Below is a clear breakdown of the cookies we set:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
doventha_session Maintains your session and workshop selections. Session (expires when you close your browser)
workshop_prefs Remembers your preferred class types and dietary notes. 30 days
analytics_uid Anonymous identifier for aggregate usage analytics (no personal data). 1 year
cookie_consent Stores your cookie preference (so we don’t bother you again). 1 year

Your choices

You have full control over cookies. Most browsers allow you to block or delete them. Here’s how:

  • Block all cookies: Adjust your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) to block third-party or all cookies.
  • Delete existing cookies: Clear your browser history / cache to remove stored cookies.
  • Withdraw consent: You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link (if available) or by clearing cookies.

Please note that blocking cookies may affect some functionality (like remembering your workshop preferences).

Third-party cookies

We do not use third-party advertising cookies. We may use embedded content (e.g., workshop videos) that sets its own cookies, but we do not control those. We recommend checking the privacy policies of those platforms (like Vimeo or YouTube) for more information.

Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology or regulations. The latest version will always be posted here with the effective date.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a news tip?

You can submit news tips through our contact form, or email us directly at tips@zenithfact.com. All tips are kept confidential.

How do I subscribe to your newsletter?

Visit our homepage and scroll to the newsletter section at the bottom. Enter your email address and click subscribe.

How can I advertise with ZenithFact?

For advertising inquiries, please contact our advertising department at ads@zenithfact.com or call (555) 123-ADVERT.

How do I report an error in a story?

If you've spotted an error, please use our contact form and select "Correction Request" from the subject dropdown.

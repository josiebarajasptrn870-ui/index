Frequently asked questions

Do you offer online baking classes?

Yes! We host monthly virtual workshops on sourdough, viennoiserie, and panettone. Check our schedule or send us a message to get the next dates.

Can I visit the studio for a private consultation?

Absolutely. We welcome bakers of all levels. Schedule a 1:1 session to troubleshoot your dough, learn shaping, or perfect your croissant lamination.

How do I order a sourdough starter kit?

Send us an email with your location, and we’ll arrange shipping. Our kits include active starter, feeding schedule, and a small bag of organic flour.
